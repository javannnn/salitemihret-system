# Training Plan

Document status: Client training plan  
Version: 1.0

## Purpose
The training plan ensures that administrators, volunteers, finance users, media users, and general users can operate the SaliteMihret System after go-live.

## Training Groups
| Group | Training Topics | Duration | Trainer | Target Date |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Super Admins | Users, roles, permissions, reports, email, system settings | 2 hours | Delivery Team | TBD |
| Finance Admins | Payments, corrections, reports, reconciliation | 1.5 hours | Delivery Team | TBD |
| PR / Office / Registrar | Members, households, import, newcomers, bilingual fields | 2 hours | Delivery Team | TBD |
| Sponsorship Committee | Sponsorship cases, pledge status, budgets, reminders | 1.5 hours | Delivery Team | TBD |
| School Admins | Sunday School/Abenet enrollment, attendance, fees, promotions | 1.5 hours | Delivery Team | TBD |
| Volunteer Coordinators | Groups, rosters, service logs, dashboards | 1 hour | Delivery Team | TBD |
| Media Admins / Kahen | Media requests, approvals, publishing, public content review | 1 hour | Delivery Team | TBD |
| Council Secretaries | Councils, trainees, minutes, quarterly reports | 1 hour | Delivery Team | TBD |
| General Users | Login, account profile, dashboard, support requests | 30 minutes | Client lead / Delivery Team | TBD |

## Training Method
- Virtual live sessions.
- Role-based walkthroughs.
- Hands-on staging exercises.
- Q&A and issue capture.
- Attendance captured in the training completion sheet.

## Training Completion Criteria
- Required sessions delivered.
- Attendance recorded.
- Users receive manuals or support guide.
- Any training questions are logged and answered or assigned.
