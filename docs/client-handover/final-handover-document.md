## Delivered System
The SaliteMihret System has been delivered as a client-facing operational platform covering:

- Dashboard and protected application shell.
- Membership records, households, imports, and member lifecycle workflows.
- Payments ledger, corrections, timelines, and finance reporting.
- Newcomer intake, follow-up, and conversion workflows.
- Sponsorship case management, pledges, budget capacity, reminders, and reporting.
- Schools, Sunday School, and Abenet workflows.
- Volunteer group, roster, and service management.
- Parish council governance records and reporting.
- Reports and exports.
- User management, roles, permissions, account management, and admin email.
- Bilingual English/Amharic data handling and website-facing acceptance.
- Public website reported as completed for client review.
